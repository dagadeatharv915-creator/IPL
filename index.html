♦About♦

         The Indian Premier League (IPL) started in the year 2008. It was introduced by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI). The main purpose of starting the IPL was to promote Twenty20 (T20) cricket and increase interest in the game. The idea of the IPL came after India won the 2007 ICC T20 World Cup. After this success, T20 cricket became very popular among fans. Seeing this popularity, the BCCI decided to launch a professional T20 league with city-based teams. The first IPL match was played on 18 April 2008 between Kolkata Knight Riders and Royal Challengers Bangalore at M. Chinnaswamy Stadium, Bengaluru. The first IPL champion was Rajasthan Royals, led by Shane Warne. Since its beginning, the IPL has grown rapidly and has become one of the most popular cricket leagues in the world, giving young players a chance to show their talent along with international players.
